Pahan Villa - Wadduwa

Pahan Villa is a Guest House which located on a beachside, Wadduwa Sri Lanka. Wadduwa has the richest cultivation of coconut palms and It's popular for products made from coconut fiber. There are some historical places like Dhaaldawatta Purana Maha Viharaya and Richmond Castle near the guest house. There are 12 Minutes away from Kaluthara Bodiya,the estuary of the Kalu Ganga, and Kalido Beach.

------------

Location

Near by Places

Pahan Villa is situated near Wadduwa beach and some popular beach sides like Waskaduwa beach, Pothupitiya Beach, Kalutara Beach, and Calido Beach Kalutara within a 1-5 Kilometers range. A few minutes away from Waskaduwa railway station of the coastal railway line Which is from Colombo fort to Belliaththa which gives wonderful public transportation through the seaside. There are many historical places near the Waskaduwa such as Richmond Castle, Kaluthara Bodiya, Daladawaththa Purana Viharaya, etc... The nearest airport Rathmalana International Airport is 24 km from the accommodation. It's 61km from the Bandaranayake International Airport Katunayake and 35km from Colombo Economic City.
